I'm t2v, a sysadmin and developer from Australia. I generally focus on self-hosting services and providing free open-source alternatives to the masses. My most used languages and frameworks are Python (fastapi, Flask). I have occasionally used Crystal (Kemal, Lucky, Onyx) and basic Typescript (React, Nodejs). My main goal with t2v is to provide free and opensource services to those in australia. Some of these include projects like t2vArchives for file/git mirrors, the only public instance of MySpotify and many FOSS frontends for popular services.
